Delete Your Valen Account

Valen allows users to permanently delete their cloud backup account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Valen app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Navigate to Cloud Backup.
  4. Sign in with your Google account.
  5. Disable Cloud Backup or remove backup data.
  6. Your cloud-stored data will be permanently deleted.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your Firebase cloud backup file (latest.json)
  • Your associated cloud backup data
  • Your Firebase authentication linkage

What Data Is Not Automatically Deleted

  • Workout data stored locally on your device
  • Local data can be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage
