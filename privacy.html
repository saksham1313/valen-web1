Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: Valen doesn't collect your data. It literally can't — there's no server on the other end. Here's the long version for the lawyers.

1. Who We Are

Valen is an Android fitness application built around the Stimulus-Recovery-Adaptation (SRA) model. It is developed and maintained as an independent product. You can reach us at support@valenapp.tech.

2. What Data We Collect

Nothing. Valen operates entirely on your device. All data you enter — workouts, sets, reps, RPE values, notes — is stored locally in your app's private storage. It does not leave your device under any circumstances.

Specifically, Valen does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, age, gender)
  • Health or fitness metrics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports (currently)

3. No Accounts, No Login

Valen requires no account, no email address, and no login. There is no user profile associated with your data on any server because there is no server.

4. Local Storage Only

All workout data, fatigue models, recovery states, and settings are stored locally on your device using Android's standard local storage mechanisms. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete this data. We strongly recommend using the export feature before uninstalling.

5. Export & Import

Valen supports exporting your full data as a JSON file and importing it back. This export is initiated and controlled entirely by you. We have no visibility into what you export, where you send it, or what you do with it.

6. Third-Party Services

In the current version of Valen, there are no third-party SDKs, analytics providers, advertising networks, or cloud services integrated into the app.

If analytics or crash reporting are added in a future release, this policy will be updated prior to that release, and users will be notified via an in-app prompt or Play Store update notes. Any such integration will be clearly disclosed.

7. Children's Privacy

Valen is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any users, there is no data from children to protect. If you are a parent or guardian with concerns, please contact us.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, the security of your data is tied to the security of your device. We recommend enabling device encryption and using a screen lock. We have no access to your data and therefore cannot be held responsible for device-level security breaches.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in any meaningful way — particularly if any form of data collection is introduced — we will update this page and note the change date. We will not retroactively apply new data practices to existing users without clear notification.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@valenapp.tech. We're actual humans and we'll actually respond.
