Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2024

At Valen, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we handle your data.

1. Local Data Storage

Valen is designed as a local-first application. All your workout data, recovery metrics, and personal training information are stored locally on your device.

2. Personal Data Collection

Valen does not collect personal data. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your identity to use the app.

3. No Third-Party Selling

We do not sell your data. Since we do not collect your data on our servers, there is no data to sell or share with third parties.

4. Data Portability

Users may export their data manually from within the app settings. This allows you to keep a backup or move your data as you see fit.

5. Future Updates

If we add analytics or cloud sync features in the future, we will update this policy and provide clear options for you to opt-in or out.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@valen.app
